# 02 · Chart Patterns

Visual formations traders read off price charts — tested under honest, causal timestamping with costs and matched-placebo comparison.

| Pattern | Verdict | Why |
|---|---|---|
| [Head & Shoulders](head-and-shoulders.md) | ❌ | Look-ahead bias: pivots and neckline are only knowable in hindsight; timestamped causally, post-break returns are indistinguishable from placebo and flat-to-negative after cost. |
| [Candlestick Patterns](candlestick-patterns.md) | ❌ | Multiple-testing + cost: dozens of tunable named patterns guarantee in-sample winners; DSR deflates the family to ~zero and they don't beat placebo net of fees. |
| [Triangles, Flags & Wedges](triangles-flags-wedges.md) | ❌ | "Perfect-trade" stats are hindsight; the pattern zoo invites multiple-testing; crypto false-breakout rate (35–50%) + turnover cost finish them. Only a *slow channel breakout* (= trend following) survives. |
| [Harmonic Patterns](harmonic-patterns.md) | ❌ | Fibonacci tolerance bands are free parameters that fit anything; leg-labeling needs the future; no causal mechanism and no placebo-beating evidence. |
| [Elliott Wave](elliott-wave.md) | ❌ | Subjective, infinitely revisable wave counts — unfalsifiable and look-ahead; no objective rule to backtest or deflate. |
| [Wyckoff Schematics](wyckoff-schematics.md) | ❌ | Spring/upthrust labels are hindsight; range edges are crowded liquidity. (See also the [Market-Mechanics framework](../07-market-mechanics/wyckoff-accumulation-distribution.md).) |

Chart patterns are the family where **look-ahead bias does the most damage** — hindsight pattern-marking inflates "win rates" that vanish once swing points are timestamped at honest confirmation time and compared against matched-placebo entries.
